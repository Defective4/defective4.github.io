Discord Webhook privacy


I take your privacy very seriously, so I take all possible measures to ensure safety of your data!

Data storage

Webhooks sent by you are stored in my database in hashed SHA256 form (form duplicate detection) and RSA encrypted blobs, meaning that only I, the person holding the private key, can decrypt them.
Even I don't access your webhooks directly (unless you ask me to delete them), only sending my key every time I need to post my update. Thus the webhooks are decrypted only when they are needed for sending you notifications.

Data deletion

You can ask me to delete your webhook at any time! Just contact me in an e-mail message (def3ctive4@gmail.com), or via a Discord direct message (java.net.defective). Just make sure you have full access to the webhook!
